Major events

Africa Days 2018

In April 2018, HEC Paris hosted the Africa Days, an event organised by the Seed Project that brought together major actors from the food and farming industry to discuss about the African “Green Revolution 2.0”. During this event, we delivered the Seed award of the best African start-up of the year 2018

Click here to watch the video of the conference

LAB 2018

The Seed Project has been invited to participate to the 2018 edition of the LAB (the Land of African Business). The LAB collects and raises awareness of global change our lifestyles and governance of economic, academic, associative, institutional and media players in Africa.

Entreprendre avec l'Afrique

The Seed Project has been invited to participate to the 2018 edition of Entreprendre avec l'Afrique . The topic of the conference is "Feed the world, a challenge shared by France and Africa"
